TELEX AM 40/5 mg

Angiotensin Receptor Blocker + CCB
  • Indicated for essential hypertension (high blood pressure).
  • For patients requiring combination therapy for blood pressure control.
  • Supports reduction of cardiovascular risk associated with uncontrolled hypertension, as determined by the treating physician.
QR Patient Summary

Take once daily at the same time each day. Avoid grapefruit juice, limit alcohol, and don't use potassium supplements or salt substitutes unless advised by your doctor. Follow a heart-healthy, low-salt diet and monitor your blood pressure regularly.

DIABMEXA M 500/1 mg

Biguanide + Sulfonylurea
  • Indicated for the management of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus.
