Our Solution

How Biomexa Works

From QR-coded packaging to AI-powered insights — a complete connected medicine ecosystem.

Smart QR Packaging

Every medicine package is QR-coded. Patients scan to get personalized medicine info, dosage instructions, safety precautions, and AI audio guidance in their local language.

Composition Indications Audio Guidance

AI Reminder System

Automated WhatsApp/SMS reminders at scheduled dose times. Patients confirm intake with a single tap. Missed doses trigger follow-up alerts and escalation protocols.

WhatsApp SMS One-Tap Confirm

Biological Clock Timing

Circadian-aware reminders aligned with optimal dosing schedules. Blood pressure meds at bedtime, statins in the evening — personalized to each patient's biological rhythm.

Circadian Personalized Optimal Timing

Drug-Food Interaction Alerts

QR safety alerts display food-related precautions. Reminders include timing instructions like "take with food" or "avoid dairy for 2 hours." Patient education improves understanding.

Food Alerts Timing Guide Education

Safety Intelligence

Drug-Food Interaction Management

Biomexa guides patients on meals, beverages, and supplements to maximize drug effectiveness and safety.

Medicine Food Interaction Biomexa Patient Guidance
Levothyroxine Food can reduce absorption Take on an empty stomach before breakfast. Reminder: "Take at 6:00 AM with water. Wait 30 min before eating."
Tetracycline Antibiotics Dairy products reduce absorption Avoid milk or calcium-rich foods close to dose. QR alert: "Avoid dairy within 2 hours of this dose."
Warfarin Vitamin K affects anticoagulation Maintain consistent leafy green intake. Dashboard flags sudden dietary changes for clinician review.
Metformin Food affects GI tolerance Take with meals if prescribed. Audio: "Take with your evening meal to reduce stomach upset."
Statins Grapefruit may increase drug levels Avoid grapefruit products when advised. Reminder includes food timing for optimal cholesterol control.
💡

Real-World Example

A patient prescribed doxycycline scans their QR package. The system says: "Avoid taking this medicine with milk or calcium supplements within 2 hours of the dose." Their 8:00 AM reminder reads: "Take doxycycline with water. Avoid dairy products around this dose." Result: Improved drug absorption and better treatment effectiveness.
